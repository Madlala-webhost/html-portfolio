My top 3 movies of all

Interstellar

If you are a fan of physics and space, this movie is just for you.

Ex Machina

The Turing test is the final barrier this humanoid machine has to cross before it walks amongst society.

Edge of Tomorrow

Tom Cruise stars in the battlefiled film where he gets killed by an alien and suddenly he discovers that he is now stuck re-living the same day over and over again. This movie will keep you on the edge of your seat.
